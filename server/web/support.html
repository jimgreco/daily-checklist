Getting Help

If sync stops working, open Account and use Sync now. If changes are still pending, keep the app open on a stable connection and try again.

Account Requests

You can export your synced data or delete your account from the Account screen. Deleting your account does not remove local offline copies from devices that are no longer connected.

Privacy Requests

Email privacy or data requests to support@jim-greco.com. Include the sign-in email for the account, but do not include sensitive checklist content in the message.

Contact

Email support requests to support@jim-greco.com and include the sign-in email for the account you need help with.
