Privacy

Daily

Data Daily Stores

Daily stores your account identity, checklist items, groups, completion history, reminder settings, and sync metadata. If you sign in with Google or Apple, Daily stores the email address and display name returned by that provider.

Local Storage

The iOS app keeps checklist data on your device so it works offline. The web app caches checklist data in browser storage for the same reason. Do not use Daily for highly sensitive information unless you are comfortable with that local-device storage model.

Export and Deletion

Signed-in users can export their synced checklist data or delete their synced account from the account screen. Deleting the account removes server-side account, checklist, identity, and session records.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, use the support page linked from the account screen.
