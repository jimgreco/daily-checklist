Daily is a focused checklist for repeatable routines.

Use it for morning tasks, household reminders, weekly planning, and simple habits that need to stay visible without turning into a full project manager.

Features:
- Daily, weekday, weekend, and custom schedules
- Groups for organizing routines
- Per-task reminders
- Evening unfinished-task check-in
- Offline-first iPhone experience
- Sync with the Daily web app when signed in
- Data export and account deletion from Account

Daily is intentionally small and direct: open the app, see what is left, check things off, and move on.
